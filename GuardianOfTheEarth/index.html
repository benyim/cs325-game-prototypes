Guardian Of The Earth!

  • High Concept: Protect the Earth as a shower of asteroids come!
  • Theme: Asteroid Heading Towards Earth
  • Mandated Variety: Point and click shooter. Randomly generated asteroids. Arcade style game.
  • Player Experience Goals: Feel the thrill of destroying everything in sight and become a hero.
  • Gameplay: You can only point and click in this game. There is also only one level. The game will later have level progress, and possibly power ups for the player.
  • Prototype Goal: Point and click shooting.
  • Features: Using your mouse to shoot at the objects. Also the feature of protecting the center of the screen (Earth) being surrounded by enemies.
  • Setting/Premise: It is far into the future, and random asteroids have been falling towards Earth lately. You are sent out to protect Earth from the shower of asteroids.
  • Story: You don't know what has been causing the asteroids...but maybe you will find out soon.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who used to be a fan of Space Invaders or any other arcade game of the sort.
  • Play Time: 1 minute to as long as you would like.
  • Strategies: The only thing that might help a player is a mouse he/she is comfortable with.
