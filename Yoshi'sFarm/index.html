Yoshi's Farm!

  • High Concept: Collect all of Yoshi's Eggs
  • Theme: Owner of a pet shop
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Drag the eggs to its coordinated crate. Randomness: a lot of randomness. Play Styles: Collector. Genres: puzzle.
  • Player Experience Goals: Being quick to collect the eggs.
  • Gameplay: Clicking and dragging the eggs to its specific crate. Each egg has a color that matches with its crate.
  • Prototype Goal: Collecting eggs by using the mouse to click and drag.
  • Features: Collect eggs by clicking and dragging them.
  • Setting/Premise: You own a pet Yoshi farm. You sell eggs to people who want to raise their own pet Yoshi.
  • Story: One day at the farm, Yoshi knocks over the crates holding the eggs and you must quickly gather them before they get cold.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who likes Yoshi and likes matching games.
  • Play Time: As long as it takes to collect all the eggs.
  • Strategies: Being good at using a mouse.
