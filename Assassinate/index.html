Sneeky Ninja!

  • High Concept: Destroy the slimes by using the help of your invisibility.
  • Theme: Invisible
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Keyboard for movement, attacking, and becoming invisible. Randomness: deterministic. Play Styles: Achiever. Genres: Overhead.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the player to feel like a ninja.
  • Gameplay:
  • Movement: WASD
  • Attack: K-key
  • Invisibility: L-key
  • The slimes can kill you if you stand in front of its path.
  • The slimes cannot touch you when you are invisible.
  • You cannot attack the slimes while being invisible.
  • Prototype Goal: The use of the keyboard to move and attack.
  • Features: The invisibility key that changes the gameplay.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a ninja who goes to the field to train but find slimes hanging around.
  • Story: You must destroy the slimes to begin training again.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who likes stealth games and dreams to be a ninja.
  • Play Time: A couple minutes or however long the player takes to win.
  • Strategies: Being patient and taking it slow.
