Clean It Up!

  • High Concept: Clean up the river that is constantly being littered with trash.
  • Theme: Preservation
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Clickable buttons to remove the trash. Randomness: a lot of randomness. Play Styles: Collector. Genres: Overhead.
  • Player Experience Goals: To be somewhat challenged with collecting the trash.
  • Gameplay:
  • Mouse: Click the trash going down the river to remove them.
  • Can is 20 points.
  • Rope is 30 points.
  • Bottle is 40 points.
  • Bag is 50 points.
  • Radioactive Waste is 100 points.
  • Prototype Goal: The use of the mouse to click on objects.
  • Features: Trash that move at higher speeds give higher points.
  • Setting/Premise: You are given a task to clean up a river.
  • Story: Your day job is cleaning up a river.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who likes point and click games.
  • Play Time: For as long as the player can last.
  • Strategies: Using an actual mouse will be helpful, as with a trackpad it is harder to click the objects.

    • Water Background: Water
    • All other assets made by me.
