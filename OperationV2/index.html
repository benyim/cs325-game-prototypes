Operate!

  • High Concept: Match the organs to the patients!
  • Theme: Organ Donor
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Click the cards and find the matching pairs. Randomness: deterministic. Play Styles: Collector. Genres: puzzle.
  • Player Experience Goals: To think by finding the matching card.
  • Gameplay: Clicking on the cards.
  • Prototype Goal: Matching cards by using the mouse to click.
  • Features: Match cards with the body by clicking on them.
  • Setting/Premise: You are an assistant to a doctor and performing in an operation.
  • Story: Help the doctor find the missing organs to the body by matching the correct organs with each other.
  • Target Audience: People who liked Operation and likes matching card games.
  • Play Time: As long as it takes to match the cards.
  • Strategies: A good memory.
