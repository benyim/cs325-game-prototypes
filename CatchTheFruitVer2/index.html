Fruit Frenzy!

  • High Concept: Catch all of the falling fruit.
  • Theme: My own theme: falling objects, fruits
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Using the mouse to move the crate Randomness: a lot of randomness. Play Styles: Collector. Genres: puzzle.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players must be quick
  • Gameplay: Move around the crate to catch all the falling fruit. But be careful to not catch the rotten fruit.
  • Prototype Goal: Using the mouse to move around the crate.
  • Features: Collect the fruits by moving the crate.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a farmer and the fruits you collected have fallen over. Quickly collect them before they fall on the ground and rot.
  • Story: An earthquake causes the fruit you have been gathering to topple over.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who likes collecting games.
  • Play Time: Play time can expand forever.
  • Strategies: Having good reflexes and an eye for which fruit is good and bad.
